Fire palace is a representative public place integrating traditional folk culture, fire temple culture and food culture in Changsha, Hunan. In particular, the flavor snacks of fire Palace are famous in Sanxiang. As a famous characteristic scenic spot in Changsha, Hunan Province, it is located in Pozi street, Changsha.

Huogong palace is also a well-known "Chinese time-honored brand" enterprise at home and abroad. You can eat all kinds of Changsha and Hunan snacks here, such as Changsha stinky tofu, authentic braised meat, Ciba and so on.


Changsha is one of the first batch of national famous historical and cultural cities, known as the "famous city of Chu and Han Dynasties". There are historical sites such as Mawangdui Han tomb, Siyang fangzun, Wu bamboo slips of the Three Kingdoms, Yuelu Academy, Tongguan kiln and so on.


Yuelu Mountain has a unique geographical location. Yuelu Mountain belongs to Hengshan Mountain in the south. The ancients listed Yuelu Mountain as the last peak of 72 peaks in the south, called Linglu peak.



When Mao Zedong was young, he studied in Changsha No. 1 normal school. He often went to Yuelu Academy with his classmates and met with Cai Hesen at the aiwan pavilion to talk about the current situation and explore the truth.
