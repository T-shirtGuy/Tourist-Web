The temple of Heaven Park is in the south of Beijing, east of yongdingmennei street, Dongcheng District. It covers an area of about 2.73 million square meters. The temple of heaven was built in 1420, the 18th year of Yongle in the Ming Dynasty.

It was rebuilt during the reign of Qianlong and Guangxu in the Qing Dynasty. It is now a world cultural heritage.In the Ming and Qing Dynasties, the temple of Heaven Park was a place for emperors to worship the emperor and pray for grain harvest.


Located in the north of China and the north of the North China Plain, Beijing is adjacent to Tianjin in the East and Hebei in the rest. It is a world-famous ancient capital and modern international city. It is also the office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.


The Forbidden City in Beijing is the imperial palace of the Ming and Qing Dynasties, formerly known as the Forbidden City. It is located in the center of the central axis of Beijing. Centered on the three main halls.



The buildings in the Forbidden City in Beijing are divided into two parts: the outer court and the inner court. The center of the outer Dynasty is Taihe hall, Zhonghe hall and Baohe hall.
